About My Research

My research is focused on advancing the fields of mathematical methods for computation, algorithms and complexity theory, parallel algorithms, and high-performance computing.

I work extensively in both numeric and symbolic mathematics using tools such as Fortran, C, Python, Julia, MATLAB, SageMath, and Maple. My work is deeply integrated with advanced and customized operating systems including Linux, FreeBSD, and DragonFlyBSD.

FreeBSD for HPC

I have authored a comprehensive book titled FreeBSD Customization for High-Performance Computing , which is available for free download. It offers in-depth guidance on tailoring FreeBSD for computationally demanding tasks.
